\section{Conclusion}

Skeleton based human action recognition is drawing more and more attention as
the popularity of 3D skeleton data. By treating skeleton sequences as images,
we propose a novel CNN based framework for both action classification and
detection tasks. Our method achieves new state-of-the-art performance on two
recent large-scale skeleton datasets. The proposed action detection approach
detects actions in a batch-processing way, while online detection is required
for real-time applications. We leave it as future exploration.
